Welcome to Manoa Bird Watch

Your gateway to discovering the vibrant birdlife of University of Hawaii Manoa Campus

Spotted Birds on Campus

Apapane

A vibrant red honeycreeper commonly seen in Manoa's forests.

Location: Manoa Falls Trail

White-rumped Shama

Famous for its melodious calls, often found near dense vegetation.

Location: Manoa Cliffs Trail

Rose-ringed Parakeet

Bright green parakeet, often seen flying in groups.

Location: Lyon Arboretum
